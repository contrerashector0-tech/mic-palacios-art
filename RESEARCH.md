## 13. Website Requirements Summary for Brand

Based on all research, the website needs to:

### Must-Have Features:
1. **Art gallery/portfolio section** — showcase her work with full-image viewing
2. **Commission request form** — structured intake (type of piece, size, subject, reference photos, budget, timeline)
3. **Print request/inquiry section** — ability to inquire about prints of existing work (with note that originals/prints may also be found at El Paso's Finest gallery)
4. **Contact section** — direct contact (email/DM redirect) for general inquiries

### Design Direction:
- **Dark color scheme** — near-black backgrounds, consistent with "Dark Shadows" brand
- **Gothic Blackletter display font** — for headers, matching her Instagram brand
- **High-contrast imagery** — her art should pop against dark backgrounds
- **Mobile-first** — her audience primarily uses Instagram (mobile)
- **Elegant, not cheesy** — gothic can look cheap if overdone. Aim for gallery-quality dark elegance
- **Cultural identity** — incorporate subtle nods to El Paso / Latinx border culture (warm earth tones as accents alongside the gothic palette?)

### Nice-to-Have:
- Events calendar (for upcoming markets / Comic Con / festivals)
- Social media feed embed (Instagram)
- Artist bio / origin story page
- Testimonials from commissioners
