## 1. Shipping Costs for Art Prints

### What Small Art Businesses Typically Charge

Most independent artists charge **$5–$15 for small prints** and **$12–$25 for larger prints**.

| Print Size | Packaging Type | Actual Ship Cost (USPS) | What to Charge Customer |
|---|---|---|---|
| 8×10 | Rigid flat mailer | $4–$7 | $6–$9 |
| 11×14 | Rigid flat mailer | $6–$9 | $9–$12 |
| 16×20 | Flat mailer or small box | $8–$12 | $12–$15 |
| 18×24 | Tube or large flat mailer | $10–$15 | $14–$18 |
| 24×36 | Mailing tube | $12–$18 | $16–$22 |

*Estimates based on USPS Zone 4–6 from El Paso, TX, ~1–2 lbs packaged.*

### Flat Rate vs. Calculated Shipping

**Flat Rate (Recommended for starting out)**
- Simple, predictable, no surprises for customer
- May slightly over/under-charge depending on destination

**Calculated/Real-Time Rates**
- Perfectly accurate but more complex to set up
- Can cause cart abandonment if shipping looks high

### Best Carrier: USPS Ground Advantage
- Cheapest option for prints (2–5 days delivery)
- 2% rate decrease in 2026
- Free supplies for Priority Mail
- Free pickup available
- Use **Pirateship.com** for 10–15% cheaper commercial rates

### Free Shipping Strategy
- Bake shipping into print price for "free shipping" (customers love it)
- Or offer **free shipping over $75–$100** to increase average order size

## 2. Packaging

### Materials Needed
- **Rigid/Stay-Flat Mailers** — prevents bending (main packaging)
- **Acid-free clear sleeve or glassine paper** — protects print surface
- **Cardboard backing board** — adds stiffness
- **"Do Not Bend" / "Fragile" stickers**
- **Mailing tubes** — for 18×24 and larger

### Supply Costs
| Item | Approx. Cost |
|---|---|
| Rigid flat mailers (pack of 25) | $12–$15 (~$0.50 each) |
| Rigid flat mailers (pack of 100) | $30–$45 (~$0.35 each) |
| Mailing tubes 2"×24" (pack of 25) | $20–$30 (~$1 each) |
| Acid-free glassine sheets (100) | $10–$15 |
| "Do Not Bend" stickers (500) | $8–$10 |

## 3. Square Checkout Shipping Setup

### For Square Payment/Checkout Links:
1. Go to **Square Dashboard → Items & Orders → Fulfillment**
2. Select **Shipping** → create a shipping rate profile
3. Choose **Flat Rate** → enter fixed amount (e.g., $9.00)
4. Enable **"Collect shipping address"** on checkout link settings
5. Link the shipping profile to your payment links

### Real-Time Carrier Rates (Advanced)
- Available through Square Online Store
- Pre-negotiated USPS and DHL rates built in
- Requires accurate item weights/dimensions
- More complex — save for later
